What To Expect

Learn The Piano!

NYPPE hosts weekly training sessions for members to practice and hone their piano playing skills. Members are split into groups based on skill level, catering to beginners and experienced players alike!

Perform!

Get opportunities to show off your talent to the rest of NYP! NYPPE hosts regular internal and public Performances for members to gain experience and showcase their skills

Meet Other Pianists

Meet and learn from other Pianists in NYPPE! You will get to meet Pianists of all genres, skillsets, and personalities!
