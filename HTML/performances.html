2021 Recital

The 2021 Recital was our first performance since the beginning of the pandemic. Despite the challenges, our members showcased their talents and dedication to music, practicing and recording their pieces remotely.

The recital features a variety of pieces, ranging from classical to pop, highlighting the diverse skills of our ensemble members. The main goal of our performance was to showcase our member's resilience against adversity, with our members having practiced hours a day during lock down.
